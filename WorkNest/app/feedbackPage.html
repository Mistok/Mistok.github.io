Appreciation

Lots of people`s voices

Because this is the People`s Talmud we have a lot of wonderful people who have volunteered as editors and proof readers. This means that many entries on Result Pages will have different styles. Sometimes even different spellings like Shabbat or Shabbos. We`re cool with that. In fact it remind us of the joke you might enjoy. A mother buys her son for his birthday two ties, one red and one blue. To please his mom, the son run upstairs and puts on the blue tie to show her nice it looks. When he comes down she only complains, "What the matter? You didn`t like the red tie?" So, you choice - Shabbat or Shabbos.
